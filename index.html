01The Product
Function

Atmospheric protection

TUBOH houses the hand-rolled cigarette, shielding it from wind, rain, and snow during use. Four nitrile O-rings deliver real hermetic sealing — not partial.

Design

Haeckel / Voronoi aesthetic

The PA12 HP Multi Jet Fusion shell features an organic pattern inspired by the natural structures documented by Ernst Haeckel. Structure and identity, fused.

Sustainability

Reusable & compostable

Built to last decades. Packaging made from Pleurotus ostreatus mycelium and hemp fiber — 100% compostable in 45 days. Produced by L-IFE S.r.l.

Target

Conscious RYO smokers

For those who roll their own and live outdoors — mountains, coast, festivals, van life. A premium accessory you buy once. The opposite of disposable culture.
